## Resource Radar Section

Title:

> See the market before your agent pays

Body:

Trust402 turns scattered x402 endpoints into a live resource map. Each resource can be checked for price, availability, policy fit, proof support, and previous receipt evidence before a paid call is made.

Cards:

- Discover: Find candidate x402 resources and normalize their metadata.
- Compare: Rank endpoints by price, uptime, proof support, and risk signals.
- Decide: Enforce allowlists, spend caps, and proof requirements before payment.
- Record: Store receipts and audit bundles for every approved action.

## Policy Section

Title:

> Autonomy with visible limits

Body:

Trust402 can act on a goal, but every paid step passes through a machine-readable spend policy. Per-call caps, per-job caps, daily budgets, allowlists, deny rules, and proof requirements are evaluated before execution.

Status labels:

- Allowed
- Review needed
- Blocked
- Receipt required
- Proof required

## Proof And Receipts Section

Title:

- Policy decision
- Proof receipt
- Final report
